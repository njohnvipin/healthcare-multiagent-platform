HealthSure Gold 2026 Member Policy

MRI Coverage:
MRI scans are covered when medically necessary and ordered by an in-network provider. MRI is subject to annual deductible and coinsurance. After the deductible is met, the plan pays 80% and the member pays 20% coinsurance.

Deductible:
Annual individual deductible is $500. The member must pay eligible expenses until the deductible is met.

Coinsurance:
After deductible is met, covered diagnostic imaging services are paid at 80% by the plan and 20% by the member.

Out-of-pocket Maximum:
Annual out-of-pocket maximum is $5,000 for in-network covered services.

Exclusions:
Experimental procedures, cosmetic procedures, and services without required prior authorization may not be covered.
