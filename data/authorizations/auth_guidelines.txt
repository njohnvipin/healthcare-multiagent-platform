HealthSure Prior Authorization Guidelines 2026

MRI Prior Authorization:
MRI scans require prior authorization when performed as an outpatient advanced imaging procedure. Approval requires documentation of medical necessity, physician order, symptoms, previous conservative treatment, and relevant clinical notes.

Authorization Decision:
If prior authorization is approved before the MRI, the claim may be processed according to plan benefits. If prior authorization is missing, the claim may be denied with reason code PA_MISSING.

Urgent Review:
Urgent requests must be escalated to clinical review if documentation is incomplete or symptoms suggest immediate medical risk.
